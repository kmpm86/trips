A Curated Holiday Itinerary

New Orleans

It is my pleasure to present your curated schedule for your upcoming holiday visit to New Orleans. I have designed this itinerary to blend the city's festive magic with its deep history and culinary excellence.

Day 1: Sunday, December 14, 2025

Arrival & French Quarter Firsts

  • 10:45 AM: Arrive at MSY (Louis Armstrong Airport)
  • 12:15 PM: Arrive at The Roosevelt New Orleans

    *A note on your arrival: You will be checking in amidst the "Waldorf Wonderland," The Roosevelt's spectacular holiday lobby display.*

  • 1:30 PM: Lunch at Napoleon House

    Your New Orleans experience begins at this historic landmark, just a short walk from your hotel.

  • After Lunch: Museum Hopping on Jackson Square

    From your table, you are perfectly positioned to explore the twin Louisiana State Museums flanking the cathedral:

    • [**The Cabildo**]: The site of the Louisiana Purchase transfer.
    • [**The Presbytère**]: Exhibits on Mardi Gras & Hurricane Katrina.
  • Evening: Celebratory Cocktails & Dinner

    An evening experiencing the city's most iconic bars.

    • **Pre-Dinner Cocktails:** [Carousel Bar & Lounge]
    • **Dinner:** [Mr. B's Bistro] (a brief stroll across the street)
    • **Post-Dinner Cocktails:** [Sazerac Bar] (a perfect nightcap back at The Roosevelt)

Day 2: Monday, December 15, 2025

A Day of History & Royal Strolls

  • 9:30 AM: Breakfast at The Fountain Lounge (The Roosevelt)
  • 10:30 AM: An immersive morning at The National WWII Museum

    *Please allow a minimum of four hours for your visit.*

  • 2:30 PM: Lunch at Gumbo Shop

    Following the museum, enjoy a classic New Orleans lunch.

  • Afternoon: Stroll the French Market & Royal Street

    A leisurely afternoon to admire the world-renowned art and antique galleries.

  • 7:00 PM: Dinner reservation at Bayona (BOOKED)

Day 3: Tuesday, December 16, 2025

Culinary Arts & City Park Magic

  • 9:00 AM: Breakfast at The Fountain Lounge (The Roosevelt)
  • 10:30 AM: Your hands-on class at the New Orleans School of Cooking
  • 1:30 PM: An essential pilgrimage to Café Du Monde

    For iconic beignets and café au lait.

  • Afternoon: Explore Jackson Square & St. Louis Cathedral

    This is the perfect time to stroll the square, enjoy the performers, and step inside the magnificent cathedral.

  • Evening: Celebration in the Oaks (BOOKED)

    *Your final evening is dedicated to one of the nation's most beloved holiday light festivals. Please plan your rideshare to arrive at [New Orleans City Park] for the **6:00 PM** opening to make the most of the lights and your putt-putt game.*

Day 4: Wednesday, December 17, 2025

Final Farewells & Departure

  • 9:30 AM: Your final breakfast at The Roosevelt
  • 11:00 AM: A tour of St. Louis Cemetery No. 1

    *Please note: Check-in is at the [Basin St. Station Visitor Information Center].*

  • 1:00 PM: One last iconic New Orleans meal: lunch at Killer PoBoys
  • 3:00 PM: Return to The Roosevelt

    To relax and pack, taking advantage of your 4:00 PM late checkout.

    **3:30 PM: Depart for MSY (Louis Armstrong Airport)** for your 5:30 PM flight.
